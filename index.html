Rules

In a game of Blackjack, you play against the dealer to get as close as possible to 21 without exceeding it.
If you exceed it, you automatically lose.

Cards value

All number cards equal their number.
Faces (Jack, Queen, King) equal 10.
Aces have a value of either 1 or 11.

The Deck of Cards

We play with a single deck of 52 cards.
The deck is reset and reshuffled between each round.

Controls

Press "Hit" to get an additional card.
Press "Stand" when you're done drawing cards.

Dealer behavior

The dealer will reveal his hidden card once you're done drawing cards or if he drew a Blackjack at the beginning.
The dealer will draw more cards until he reaches at least 17.

Soft 17

A soft 17 is when your total is equal to 17 with the help of an Ace that has a value of 11.
The dealer WILL draw on a Soft 17.
